Biography

I received a master's degree in Control Science and Engineering from the Institute of Logistics Science and Engineering at Shanghai Maritime University in 2024. Currently, I am an Ph. D Student at the Traffic College, Southeast University. My interests include localization in intelligent transportation system/image processing/object detection and object tracking for transportation.
